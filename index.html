About Me

My name is Cameron Hurley. I graduated from Plymouth State University in 2011 with a Bachelors Degree in Marketing. After graduating I moved down to Boston for 4 years where I worked in various restaurant, retail, and managerial roles. I moved back to my homestate of NH in 2015 where I continued to work in sales. I have always been intruiged by the Technology field and coding in general, prompting me to enroll into a coding bootcamp to pursue a career in Web/Software Development.

I am an avid fisherman of Striped Bass and try to get out on the water in any free time I have. I also am a huge Boston sports fan with football being my favorite. I also enjoy going to the beach, hiking, travelling to new places, and hanging out with friends.
